Knowledge, ambition and innovation

HES Med Terminals

Fos-sur-Mer, French Mediterranean coast

Our exceptional geographical location makes us an ideal hub for Europe, North Africa and Asian shipping routes.

Due to our strategic location in the heart of the North Western Mediterranean at the crossroads of the maritime transport routes, we are linked to the main European capitals in less than 24 hours.

We are specialized in the handling and storage of steel products, dry bulk cargo as well as heavy lift.

One of our 3 terminals is fully dedicated to breakbulk. The development of the breakbulk activity requires innovation and engineering and that’s exactly why our breakbulk terminal is so attractive for customers.

We realize the most impressive projects; unloading of military tanks, outstanding works of Art, roro loading of trains, ITER project… We are able to manage the most exclusive and extraordinary requests.

Bulk for life at HES Med Terminals

Contributing to the world of tomorrow. That’s what we do at our terminals. We are an important switch in the continuous delivery of essential building blocks for our everyday life.

The car you drive or the energy from your socket for example: chances are that the necessary raw materials for these products were transported via our terminals.

At our terminals, around 144 colleagues work hard 24/7 on matters that affect and shape our daily lives.

They always do so with the highest level of commitment to both safety and care for the environment – essential when working for or with us.

Safety

Prevention of accidents and incidents is part of the core values and executed by employees and contractors. We believe that accidents and injuries are avoidable. We are committed to achieving our target goal of zero incidents and are mindful of the environment we live in. They are determined to excel in a strongly rooted culture of safety and operational excellence.

Quality

By caring for nature, employees and customers’ requirements, we are dedicated to maintaining the certificates on environment, safety, health, quality and security.
We are ISO 9001, 45001 and MASE certified.

EnviroNment

In the business of extensive dry bulk supply, we are alert to the potential environmental impact of the activities. That is exactly why they implement special measures to reduce dust, noise and other potential environmental impacts. A dust suppression system covers the whole storage area. If necessary, stockpiles and open areas are covered with a protective layer as a precautionary measure.
