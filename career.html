Your Career

Would you like to further your career at Hes international Europe or one of its subsidiaries?

Our company employs ± 1450 people, both outside or in the office, who work with great enthusiasm every day. With a job at Hes international Europe or one of its subsidiaries, you are assured of good future prospects and opportunities for growth. Are you passionate about technology? Or are you more a logistics kind of person? Do you enjoy working in an international setting whilst still being close to home? Do you feel at home in a dynamic working environment where people interact with each other in a personal and direct way? If this appeals to you, our company has a world full of opportunities for you as well. 

At HES, we strongly believe that diversity is paramount to driving creativity, innovation and value for people. All applications will receive consideration for employment without regard to ethnicity, religion, gender, gender identity, or expression, sexual orientation, nationality, disability, age, or social background.

Interested in an opportunity at HES? Please check our ‘Working at HES’ site. 

Young Talent TRAINEES, GRADUATES AND STARTERS

HES has a leading position in the liquid and dry bulk market in Europe. Whether you're studying engineering, HSSE, logistics or business economics, our group has a world full of opportunities to offer in various fields. By combining knowledge and experience we can take you and HES further, ensuring that we both can continue to develop and contribute to the world of tomorrow.
