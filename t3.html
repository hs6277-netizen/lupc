Knowledge, ambition and innovation

HES Gdynia Bulk Terminal

Port of Gdynia

A diversified and modern terminal at the main entrance of the Port of Gdynia

We are one of the largest dry bulk terminals in Poland, strategically located in the Port of Gdynia with flexible hinterland connections via rail and road.

The excellent water, rail and road connections combined with the outstanding handling and storage capabilities enable us to meet customer’s expectations, as well as the business challenges of the future. Continuous investments over the past years have led our a flexible and efficient terminal.

We offer open storage possibilities as well as covered storage for various dry bulk products with automated technology for incoming cargo. In addition to loading, unloading and storage, our terminal provides a direct transloading service of liquid bulk products with an option of supplying heating or nitrogen.

In the business of extensive dry bulk supply, we are alert to the potential environmental impact of the activities. That is exactly why we implement special measures to reduce dust, noise and other potential environmental impacts. Knowledge, ambition, decades of experience and our ever-growing innovation capacity makes us a competent partner to rely on, now and in the future.

Bulk for life at HES Gdynia Bulk Terminal

Contributing to the world of tomorrow. That’s what we do at our terminal. We are an important switch in the continuous delivery of essential building blocks for our everyday life.

The car you drive in, the energy to keep your house warm, the basis for your food and feed for animals; there is a big chance that the raw materials for these products have found their way via our terminal to their final destination.

At our terminal, around 140 colleagues dynamically work 24/7 on matters that affect and shape our daily lives. They always do so with the highest level of commitment to both safety and care for the environment – essential when working for or with us.

Welcome at HES Gdynia Bulk Terminal, even in these extraordinary times

The coronavirus is significantly impacting our daily lives. Both your safety and that of our employees is always our number one priority. For that reason, we closely adhere to the government measures regarding the coronavirus. We have supported local hospitals by donating a ventilator and personal protective equipment for medical staffs.

The products that we store and handle at our terminals are of vital importance to the society. In order to guarantee that these processes can continue as optimally as possible, compliance with the applicable guidelines is essential at our terminals. We are doing our best to maintain our level of service provision. Let's help each other get through these times as best as we can. Thank you for your support and understanding.

Safety

We are determined to excel in a strongly rooted culture of safety and operational excellence. Prevention of accidents and incidents is one of our core values.

We have introduced stringent standards and procedures for health and safety management which are constantly reviewed to ensure that we protect our people and our customers. We conduct regular training to give our staff the confidence to work safely and to assure that procedures are understood and respected by every employee and contractor acting at our terminal.

Quality

By caring for nature, employees and customers’ requirements, we perform our work in a safe and professional manner in conformance with regulations, terminal rules, quality standards and company policies. HES Gdynia Bulk terminal is ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, ISO 22000, AEO, GMP+  certified.

EnviroNment

In the business of extensive dry bulk supply, we are alert to the potential environmental impact of the activities. That is exactly why we implement special measures to reduce dust, noise and other potential environmental impacts. We have invested in modern systems of protecting piles against excessive dusting by covering products with a thin layer of cellulose using Dustcrusterliquid®.
