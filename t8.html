HES Bulk Terminal Maasdelta

Port of Rotterdam

The largest dry bulk terminal for agri, minerals and biomass in the Port of Rotterdam

We are a multipurpose dry bulk terminal operator with two strategically located terminals in the Port of Rotterdam. The terminals have excellent access to deep-water seaways and to the hinterland by means of inland waterways, rail links and motorways. 
Both terminals are equipped with the latest technology in the area of loading, unloading and storage facilities. HBTM not only has onshore equipment (mechanical and pneumatic) at its disposal, but also a fleet of floating cranes and weighing towers. We are a logistical partner for customers in the food and feed industry, building and production industry and the energy sector.

 HBTM is a terminal in transition and is investing heavily in new products and services to address changes in market and customer demand. To grow its services in agricultural products, minerals, building materials and biomass, high quality covered storage is required. HBTM is well on track in executing a four-year masterplan to construct a total of 350,000 cbm of additional covered storage capacity at the Laurenshaven and Europoort locations. This will increase HBTM’s total available covered storage to 800,000 cbm. These multipurpose sheds are located at storage areas previously used for the storage of in particular steam coal. Furthermore HBTM has land and berth capacity available for further expansion.

Bulk for life at HBTM

Contributing to the world of tomorrow. That’s what we do at HBTM. We are an important switch in the continuous delivery of essential building blocks for our everyday life.

Chances are that the raw materials for these consumer goods – from food to feed – have passed through HBTM’s agricultural storage and transshipment facilities.

We also offer a broad range of open and covered storage solutions for mineral products. This means we handle materials for both the steel industry and the mineral industry, providing versatile storage options suitable for most bulk commodities.

At HBTM, our team works 24/7 on matters that affect and shape our daily lives.

They always do so with the highest level of commitment to both safety and care for the environment – essential when working for or with HBTM.

Your career at HBTM?

At HES Bulk Terminal Maasdelta (HBTM), you'll find a unique and dynamic work environment. Our operations span two strategically located terminals in the Port of Rotterdam, covering extensive areas equipped with colossal cranes and a fleet of floating cranes. Every day, we handle a diverse range of dry bulk products, from minerals and agribulk to biomass, ensuring the seamless flow of essential materials that impact daily life. Our team of professionals works around the clock, embodying a strong sense of companionship and a shared commitment to safety and excellence. Joining HBTM means engaging in meaningful work with ample opportunities for personal and professional growth.

SAFETY, HEALTH, AND PORT SECURITY

At HBTM, continuous attention to these topics is central and essential to the success of our operations.

We are committed to protecting the health and safety of our employees and others affected by our business, at our locations as well as in the surrounding environment. We also ensure the safety of our facilities and infrastructure. We operate in accordance with applicable laws and regulations, and for which everyone has a responsibility and obligation to prevent incidents and their associated consequences for personnel and the company. Furthermore, we strive for continuous process improvement and awareness to further increase safety and security levels.

FEED SAFETY

At the Europoort terminal, we primarily handle agricultural products. To ensure that no contamination occurs during the handling of these products, HBTM adheres to very strict feed safety standards. Compliance is monitored through the annual GMP+ FC 2020 (FSA, FRA) audit.

Quality management

To meet the obligations of its customers, employees, and the environment, and to guarantee the quality of its core activities, HBTM has created, implemented, and maintained a management system certified according to the requirements of ISO 9001:2015, GMP+ FC 2020 (FSA and FRA including MI 5.4 for Non-GMO), and ISPS.

Environment

Caring for people and the environment plays a vital role at HBTM, not only for the people who work at our terminals, but also for our surrounding area. In addition to caring for the environment, we believe it's important that everyone works safely at our terminals.

HBTM believes that caring for the environment is a shared responsibility of both employers and employees, in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Together, we build a safe working and living environment with the aim of preventing accidents and environmental damage.

Operating hours

HBTM works 24 hours per day, from Monday 07.30 until Saturday 15.30, with the exception of the following official Holidays:

  • New Years Day (1st January)
  • Easter Sunday and Easter Monday
  • King's Day (27th April or 26th April if 27th April occurs to be a Sunday)
  • Liberation Day (5th May, every 5 years, 2025/2030 etc)
  • Ascension Day
  • Whitsunday and Whitmonday
  • 1st and 2nd Christmas Day (25th and 26th December)

Works ends from 15.30 hours of the day preceding an official Holiday through to 07.30 hours of the day following an official holiday.
